# privateGP

A telehealth application designed for use in Australian General Practice.

## Project Goals
- Provide secure video consultations and messaging between GPs and patients.
- Support appointment scheduling, patient records, and billing integrations.
- Ensure compliance with Australian healthcare regulations and privacy standards.

## Regulatory and Compliance Considerations
- Follow the [Australian Privacy Principles](https://www.oaic.gov.au/privacy/australian-privacy-principles) and the Privacy Act 1988.
- Align with guidance from the [Australian Digital Health Agency](https://www.digitalhealth.gov.au/) for telehealth services.
- Ensure any medical practitioners comply with AHPRA regulations.
